About: This website is a place for all of my work from Professor Ryan Sloan's research class. My initial focus was money, and it's involvement in technology and people; the title is coined from the popular "oh my zsh" terminal customization tool with a twist to reflect how technology—particularly computing—has evolved from a geeky profession into the lucrative field that it is known as. Here are some brief summaries of the projects:

Project 1

In this preliminary project, I discuss the speculative dynamics between humans and artificial intelligence. In particular, I explore how we might perceive such artificially intelligent systems and some scientific/expert commentary on such technologies.

Project 2

In this project, I explore various topics in preperation for project 3.

Project 3

In this final project, I dive deep into the pipeline of talent that feeds into Silicon Valley. I explore what gaps in participation that currently exist in the industry, and how these gaps might be deep rooted in the pipeline. In other words, I ask: How early does the pipeline begin? Why does it matter for STEM talent development to begin early? Who is privileged in entering the pipeline at these early stages?
